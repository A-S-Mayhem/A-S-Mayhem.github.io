SAM 2016
======
The Ninth IEEE Sensor Array and Multichannel Signal Processing Workshop. 10th-13th July 2016, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.


The [SAM Workshop](http://sam2016.cetuc.puc-rio.br/) is an important IEEE Signal Processing Society event dedicated to sensor array and multichannel signal processing. The organizing committee invites the international community to contribute with state-of-the-art developments in the field. 

The workshop was held at the Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro, located in GÃ¡vea, in a superb area surrounded by beaches, mountains and the Tijuca National Forest, the world's largest urban forest.

Rio de Janeiro is a world renowned city for its culture, beautiful landscapes, numerous tourist attractions and international cuisine. 

The workshop took place during the first half of July about a month before the 2016 Summer Olympic Games.
