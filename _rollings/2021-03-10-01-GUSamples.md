Just performed the acquisition of Hyperspectral data for 30 samples associated to geological units of a mine under explotation.

The Samples
======

Our industrial partner (Secret Agent 001, SA001) provided us 30 samples of pulverized material. The samples has been extracter from geologic units and its information is available for further analysis.
In addition, SA001 sent us some models that other technical staff has been implemented for the samples.
