This course surveys some key topics in sports analytics and their implementation using Python. The course content is inspired by many resources including books, blogs, other courses found online. 
- *Mathletics: How Gamblers, Managers, And Fans Use Mathematics in Sports*, 2nd Ed., Winston, Nestler, Pelechrinis (2022)
- *Handbook of Statistical Methods and Analyses in Sports*, Edited by Albert, Glickman, Swartz, Koning (2017)
- *Introduction to Statistical Learning: With Applications in Python*, James, Witten, Hastie, Tibshirani, Taylor (2023), https://www.statlearning.com/
- *Teaching Statistics Using Baseball*, 2nd Ed., Albert (2017)
- STAT 143 - Sports Analytics (Harvard University)
- INFSCI 1091 - Moneyball 2.0: Winning in Sports with Data (University of Pittsburgh)
- MA 276 - Statistics and Sports (Skidmore College)
