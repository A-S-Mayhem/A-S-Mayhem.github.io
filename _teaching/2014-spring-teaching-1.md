[CS 181](https://harvard-ml-courses.github.io/cs181-web/) provides a broad and rigorous introduction to machine learning, probabilistic reasoning and decision making in uncertain environments. Topics include: supervised learning, ensemble methods and boosting, neural networks, support vector machines, kernel methods, clustering and unsupervised learning, maximum likelihood, graphical models, hidden Markov models, inference methods, reinforcement learning.
