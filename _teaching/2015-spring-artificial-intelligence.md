A student assistant is a Master student who assists the lecturer of a course. This is a paid position. 

My responsibilities included: 

- Running the work groups. 
- Showing students how to solve the exercises on the blackboard.
- Assisting students during the work groups; helping them with coding, maths, experimentation.
- Code reviews and student paper reviews.
- Grading homework and student projects.
