This course is an introduction to the analysis of social media data. It introduces key concepts on capturing, cleaning and analysing social media data and working with APIs with an emphasis on social network analysis and the opportunities and limitations of social media data. The course is based on applications following the tools of the rtweet and twitteR R packages.
