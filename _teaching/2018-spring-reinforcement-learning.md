An assistant lecturer is a PhD student who assists the lecturer of a course and has responsibilities that go beyond what is typically expected of a PhD student or other teaching assistant. 

My responsibilities included: 

- Designing a 2 ECTS student project for teams of two students. 
- Writing the relevant skeleton code for this assignment.
- Teaching work groups, assisting students with theory, coding, project planning and paper writing. , 
- Grading all student projects.
