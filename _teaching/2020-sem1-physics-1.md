Course designation: 
This course provides an introduction to mechanics including: concepts and principles of kinetics, dynamics, energetics of motion of a particle and a rigid body.

Topics:
• Chapter 1: Bases of Kinematics
• Chapter 2: The Law of Motion
• Chapter 3: Work and Mechanical Energy
• Chapter 4: Linear Momentum and Collisions
• Chapter 5: Rotation of a Rigid Object About a Fixed Axis
• Chapter 6: Equilibrium and Elasticity
• Chapter 7: Universal Gravitation
