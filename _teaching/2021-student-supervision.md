I supervised the following Master students for their 18 [ECTS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/European_Credit_Transfer_and_Accumulation_System#:~:text=The%20European%20Credit%20Transfer%20and,and%20other%20collaborating%20European%20countries.) Research Project and their 42 ECTS Master Project:

- **Jeroen Rook** (September 2019 - June 2021), co-supervised with prof. dr. Holger Hoos and dr. Siegfried Nijssen. We presented an extended abstract of his exploratory work at the [International Workshop on Model Counting](https://mccompetition.org/2020/mcw_program), co-located with SAT 2020 [[pdf](https://mccompetition.org/assets/files/2020/MCW_2020_paper_10.pdf), [slides](https://mccompetition.org/assets/files/2020/MCW_2020_slides_10.pdf)]. We also presented an update of his work at the [Workshop on Counting and Sampling](https://mccompetition.org/2021/mcw_description), co-located with SAT 2021 [[pdf](https://mccompetition.org/assets/files/2021/WCS_2021_abstract_8_cachingSchemes.pdf), [slides](https://mccompetition.org/assets/files/2021/WCS_2021_slides_8_cachingSchemes.pdf), [video](https://cloudstore.zih.tu-dresden.de/index.php/s/4aRmLwHr5jZrkoi)]. See also [Jeroen’s Master thesis](https://theses.liacs.nl/2035).
- **Daniël Fokkinga**. September 2018 - December 2019, co-supervised with Marie Anastacio, prof. dr. Holger Hoos and dr. Siegfried Nijssen. See the [paper](http://ada.liacs.nl/papers/FokEtAl19.pdf) we presented at the [Data Science meets Optimisation (DSO) workshop](https://sites.google.com/view/ijcai2019dso/), co-located with IJCAI 2019, and [Daniël’s Master thesis](https://theses.liacs.nl/1698).
