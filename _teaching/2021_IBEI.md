This course provides students with an introduction to research design and methodology, with an emphasis on research techniques and analysis in the social sciences. It is intended as a broad introduction to the logic and practical skills required to understand social science research, including quantitative, qualitative and computational approaches. Basic tips for using research tools and resources aim at developing basic skills for academic research.

The course is specially targeted at students with little or no prior experience with social science research.
