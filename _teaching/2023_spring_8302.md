### Contents

Smooth manifolds, tangent spaces, embedding/immersion, Sard's theorem, Frobenius theorem, differential forms, integration,
curvature, Gauss-Bonnet theorem.
Time permitting: de Rham, duality in manifolds.
A detailed day by day plan can be found on [Canvas](https://canvas.umn.edu/courses/356310).

### Prerequisites

Knowledge of basic point-set topology and multivariate calculus will be essential for this course.
