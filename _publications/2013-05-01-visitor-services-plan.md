The VSP is a step-down management plan. It is an integrated analysis of all applicable aspects of visitor service programs on a refuge. It promotes safety of visitors and facilities, compliance with applicable laws,manages conflicts with the goals of the Refuge system and wildlife-dependent recreation, provides opportunities to experience wildlife, implements methods to define and evaluate visitor programs and experiences, and promotes stewardship and conservation.
