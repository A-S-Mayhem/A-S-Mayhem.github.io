The paper discusses the importance of understanding the intent and potential harm associated with viral memes. It focuses on identifying the roles of entities within memes, such as &apos;hero,&apos; &apos;villain,&apos; or &apos;victim.&apos; The study introduces a multi-modal framework called VECTOR for this task, which outperforms standard models. The research also highlights challenges in semantically labeling roles within memes and provides comparative analyses.
