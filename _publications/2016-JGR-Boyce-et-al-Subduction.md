[Key Points]: Body wave tomography shows a systematic decrease in wave speed across tectonic boundaries in SE Canada. A distinct vertical wave speed boundary is imaged directly beneath the Grenville Front. A metasomatized craton edge best explains the vertical wave speed boundary
