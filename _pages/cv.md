Work experience
======
* July 2021 - December 2022: Research Engineer at Umeå University & WASP Research Arena for Media and Language
  * Duties included: Design, implement and maintain the machine learning platform for WARA Media and Language. Collection and provision of data, coomunication with the industry and academic partners regarding platform development, assist with the annotation services and provide benchmarking to machine learning challenges (GENEA 2022 and VOT Challenge)
  * Supervisor: [Prof Johanna Björklund](https://www.umu.se/personal/johanna-bjorklund/)
* September 2020 - June 2021: Research Intern and Master Thesis Student
  * Carried out Master thesis project in [Analysis of visual political communication on YouTube](http://uu.diva-portal.org/smash/record.jsf?pid=diva2:1574931), as a part of the project on Machine Learning and political communication.
  * Developed deep learning methods using transfer learning for the analysis of image-based online communication.
  * Developed a custom image classifier to detect object based labels related to climate change using transfer learning.
  * Performed information extraction from video frames using the state of the art models like Google's Vision API, Tesseract-OCR, and IBM Watson
  * Established a benchmark model by comparing the results with the state-of-the-art models. 
  * Automated classification of video frames taken from YouTube videos about climate change using the benchmark model.
  * Supervisor: [Prof Matteo Magnani](https://www.katalog.uu.se/profile/?id=N13-1077)
* September 2020 - March 2021: Graduate Teaching Assistant at Uppsala University
  * Supported IT and Engineering students for the Data Mining course during Autumn 2020 and Spring 2021.
  * Provided assistance to students in executing different Data mining concepts (Data preprocessing, Classification, Clustering, and Association Rule Mining) using the RapidMiner tool.
  * Monitored project teams and helped them with their progress. Reviewed project reports and provided suggestions.
  * Reported a short summary to the professor regarding the major obstacles faced by the students during the project.
  * Supervisor: [Prof Matteo Magnani](https://www.katalog.uu.se/profile/?id=N13-1077)
