Post-Rebel Electoral Parties (PREP) Dataset

A dataset of national-level elections of post-rebel electoral parties (1990-2021). The current version of the data consists of 78 distinct parties derived from 56 conflict actors. The data cover 322 legislative election years and 216 executive elections in 39 countries over 30 years.

Coalitions in Electoral Authoritarian Regimes (CEAR) Dataset
