Previously (2011-2014) at Loughborough University I was involved in developing and maintaining software to model physical processes, such as during radiation damage, occurring during radiation damage events.



In order to model such events, two in-house software packages were exploited. LboMD was used to model the ballistic phase and LAKMC was used to model the recovery phase. To read more about the software and the project, please check my PhD thesis.
