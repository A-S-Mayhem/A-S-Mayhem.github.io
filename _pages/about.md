I am a first-year PhD student at Northwestern University located in Chicago, Illinois. Prior to joining NU, I was working as a Data engineer at [Bank OZK](https://www.ozk.com/personal/), Innovation Labs. I worked at [Precima](https://precima.com/), a Nielsen company located in Chicago as a Data Scientist Intern. I graduated with Master's in Data Analytics and Security from University of South Florida located in Tampa, Florida. 
My research interests are Ubiquitous Computing, Human Activity Recognition, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.
