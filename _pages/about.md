I am a professor in the Department of Ecology & Vertebrate Zoology, University of Łódź, Poland. I alos have the role of Knowledge Transfer Leader at the Centre for Data Analysis, Modelling and Computational Sciences (CAMINO) at the University of Łódź.

My research and teaching focus on evolution and ecology, and I also have an interest in statistics. 

I am Editor-in-Chief of the Journal of Vertebrate Biology, published by the Institute of Vertebrate Biology of the Czech Academy of Sciences in Brno, Czech Republic.
