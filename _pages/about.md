Hi! I am currently a postgraduate student at [Soochow University](http://www.suda.edu.cn) in the [School of Computer Science & Technology](http://scst.suda.edu.cn/) . My supervisor is Prof. [Jin Wang](http://web.suda.edu.cn/wjin1985/index.html). Before that, I received double B.S. degrees in computer science from Northern Arizona University & computer science and technology from Yangzhou University in 2020, respectively. My research interests include coded computing, blockchain security and distributed systems.<br>
Feel free to discuss blockchain related issues with me!
