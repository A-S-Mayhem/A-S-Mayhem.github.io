I am an incoming Masters student at University of California San Diego in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department. I will be specalising in Intelligent systems, Robotics and Control. I mostly work in the areas of Computer Vision, Reinforcement learning its applications. I finished my Bachelors of Technology in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad [IITH](https://iith.ac.in/). During my time there I was part of the [Pranet Lab](https://pranet.iith.ac.in/) working with Prof. Kotaro Kataoka. I have also had a chance to work with Prof. Summohana Channappayya and Prof. Kiran Kuchi during my undergraduate study.
