Computational Neuroethology

## add some tiny behavior-brain icon
1. Computational neurethological basis of primate grooming: this project combines neural modeling, imitation learning, and computer vision to develop a computational framework describing how primates use social touch to communicate.

AI for Health and Wellness

## add some tiny health-related icon 1. Foraging and ADHD: this project explores the potential of an explore-exploit decision making task as a biomarker for ADHD diagnoses. With: David Barack, Vera Ludwig

2. So You Think AI Can Dance? This project aims to decompose myriad dance genre through novel self-supervised approaches. With: Ben Baker
