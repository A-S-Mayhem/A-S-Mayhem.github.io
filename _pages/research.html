Here is the archive of talks: CARGO archive. Feel free to subscribe to our mailing list. Let me also mention the archive of our Theory Seminars talks on Fridays. I have given four talks whose abstract is available there.

Sometimes I find enough time and I write the notes of talks I give in CARGO. You can find them here: Mathematical Scribblings.
