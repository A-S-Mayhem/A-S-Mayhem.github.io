Due to the lack of an open source working example of an inductive training example of [Deep Graph Infomax](https://arxiv.org/pdf/1809.10341.pdf) I implemented one in Pytorch. This was then merged into the [Pytorch Geometric](https://github.com/rusty1s/pytorch_geometric) library.
