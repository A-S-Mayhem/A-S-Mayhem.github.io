The Data Analysis, Activism, Advocacy, and Applied Mathematics Network is a group working to apply data science and applied mathematics techniques to promote social justice.  This group began with a 4-week program at ICERM in the summer of 2022.

<!--end-excerpt-->

I helped/will help co-organize several of the events below.

##### Events

* [Data Science and Social Justice: Networks, Policy, and Education](https://icerm.brown.edu/programs/ep-22-dssj/), ICERM, June 13 - July 8, 2022
* Workshop: [Interdisciplinary Network Analysis Methods for Analyzing Social Systems](https://icerm.brown.edu/programs/ep-22-dssj/w1/), ICERM, June 27 -- July 1, 2022
* [Interdisciplinary and Critical Data Science Motivated by Social Justice](https://www.imsi.institute/activities/data-science-motivated-by-social-justice/), IMSI, January 9 -- February 3, 2023
* [Data Science and Social Justice: Networks, Policy, and Education (Part II)](https://icerm.brown.edu/programs/ep-23-dssj/), ICERM, June 20 -- July 28, 2023
* [From Impact Factor to Influence Factor: Data Science and Policy for Social Justice Workshop](https://icerm.brown.edu/programs/ep-23-dssj/w1/), ICERM, June 26 -- June 30, 2023
* [Educating at the Intersection of Data Science and Social Justice Workshop](https://icerm.brown.edu/programs/ep-23-dssj/w2/), ICERM, July 17 -- July 21, 2023
