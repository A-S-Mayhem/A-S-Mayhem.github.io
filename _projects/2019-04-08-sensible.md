SENSIBLE (full name crossMedia crossLingual Knowledge extraction) is an ongoing H2020 Marie-Curie RISE project focused on 
developing a novel integrated decision support mechanism embedding intelligent sensing, communications and data processing methodology 
for improving sustainability of smart buildings. 

The project is coordinated by our collegue [Prof. Vladimir Stankovic](https://www.strath.ac.uk/staff/stankovicvladimirdr/) from the 
University of Strathclyde, UK.

For more information checkout the [SENSIBLE website](https://sensible.eee.strath.ac.uk/objectives/).
