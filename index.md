### **About Me / 自己紹介**


Currently, I'm interested in modeling techniques and algorithms on mixed-integer optimization and related problems. In particular, solving mixed-integer conic optimization problems is one of my ongoing research projects. I am also interested in applications of mathematical optimization to other engineering fields such as machine learning, production planning, and so on.
