Yummy tangy lemon loaf with poppyseeds and icing sugar glaze

![Lemon Poppyseed Loaf](/images/Recipes_Lemon-Loaf.jpg)

###### Ingredients - Loaf

- Â¾ cup sugar
- 1 1/4 cup flour
- 1/3 cup margarine
- 1 tbsp poppyseed
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 2 tbsp lemon rind
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 3 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/3 cup sour cream
- 1/3 cup milk

###### Ingredients - Glaze

- 1/4 cup icing sugar
- 2 tbsp lemon juice


###### Instructions

1. In a mixing bowl, add all dry ingredients
2. Add in egg, milk, sour cream, lemon juice, and rind. Mix together well.
3. Line a loaf pan with parchment paper.
4. Preheat oven to 350F
5. Add batter to loaf pan. Bake for 35-40 minutes
6. Mix together sugar and lemon juice for glaze
7. After baking prick loaf with a fork and pour glaze over loaf
