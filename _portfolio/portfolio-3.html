--- title: "Brain Implantable Device" excerpt: "Brain Implantable Device fabricated using Polymide Dupont Pyralux AP8535R and powered using Wireless Power Transmission for wireless power and communication. Device encapsulated with biocompatible material.
" collection: portfolio --- Design and fabrication of an stretchable and miniaturized brain implantable device following a modular structure approach to the design. Implemented stages are antenna, conditioner circuit and shank. The implant was fabricated using a flexible substrate of Polyimide and encapsulated by PDMS for chronic implantation. Finite element method was run on COMSOL Multiphysics for mechanical simulation.
